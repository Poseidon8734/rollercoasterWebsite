Fun Facts!

The first rollercoaster was only 50 feet tall and had a max speed of 6 mph.

The fastest is Formula Rossa in Ferrari World, Abu Dhabi, UAE at 149 MPH.

Early roller coasters were inspired by 17th century Russian ice slides.

The one with the most loops is The Smiler at Alton Towers in Staffordshire, UK.

People used to ride rollercoasters sideways.

The tallest is Kingda Ka in Six Flags Great Adventure in Jackson, New Jersey, at 456 feet.
